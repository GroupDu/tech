First, send an email to ITSC to explain your purpose and get "Tenant ID" and "Application ID". Then, we take Manjaro Linux as an example.

  1. Install Evolution and its ews plugin: "sudo pacman -S evolution evolution-ews"
  2. Open Evolution
  3. Click "Edit - Preferecnces - Add email account"
  4. Input your email address: "xxx@connect.ust.hk", then click "Next"
  5. Choose "Server type" as "Exchange Web Services", then input the "Host Url": "https://outlook.office365.com/EWS/Exchange.asmx"
  6. Then choose the authentication type as "OAuth2", and input the "Tenant ID" and "Application ID"
  7. Click "Next" until "Apply", and it will prompts a CAS authentication window, just authenticate with your Duo Mobile App.
  8. Done.
